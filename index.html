About Me

Hi, I'm Steve Longoria! I first became interested in computers when my step-father bought me a Commodore 64 in the early 90s. In the late 90's is when we bought our first desktop computer that was connected to the Internet! I think our ISP was NetZero. Needless to say, I was transfixed! :)

I created my first static HTML website in my late teens and then quickly discovered blogging in my early 20s. The first blog I created was a music blog called RisingFist.com where I wrote about and filmed some of my favorite bands. I managed that blog for roughly 8 years before I put it down and moved on to other projects. At its peak, RisingFist.com was getting about 500 visitors a day. I didn't try too hard to monetize that blog as it was more of a labor of love.

That experience in blogging helped me land my first client. I helped drive traffic to his site, increase email sign ups and make more money in general from his site. From there I was hooked. I discovered I loved helping businesses succeed via the Internet.

Over the next several years I've helped several small to medium sized businesses with their digital marketing and lead generation. Check out this post to see one of my most recent testimonials/case studies!

These days you'll find me working as a freelance business consultant. I also blog and publish content in various formats. My favorite business books are Rework, Ogilvy On Advertising and Badass: Making Users Awesome, just to name a few.
