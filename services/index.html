FAQ

Will you write new Google Ad copy every month?

I will write new copy as the opportunity presents itself. I can’t guarantee X new ads per month as it is a creative process. When I see opportunities to increase your conversion rate by testing out new ad copy I will. Coming up with ad copy and offers is a collaborative process that involves me and you, the client.

Do you charge a percentage of my monthly ad spend?

Nope, my prices are fixed so you can scale a successful campaign without having to worry about paying me more money every month.

Do you charge a set up fee?

Nope, you simply pay for your first month of service and I include the set up of your Google Ad campaign as well as landing page development and sales copywriting if you choose the more premium packages.

Can I cancel at anytime?

Yes, this service is month-to-month. There are no long-term contracts involved so you can cancel at any time.

Do you perform the work on my Google Ad account?

Yes, I believe you should get to keep the work you pay me to do. This means that I build your Google Ad campaign using your Google Ad Account, not mine. If we ever part ways, you get to keep the work I did.
