    Genie Electric has been serving the Central Oregon area since 1972. There are many projects in the area that we have been involved with, including: The original Riverhouse Motel and Restaurant, River's Edge Golf Proshop, Waterside Commercial Building and others. Residential projects include: River's Edge single-lot-line and custom housing in phases 1,2,and 3 as well as FHA tract housing in Bend. In recent years our company has down-sized to a one-man owner/operator shop specializing in residential and commercial maintenance and repair, remodeling and one-on-one design/build projects with the building contractor and property owner.
