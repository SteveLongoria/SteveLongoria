    Woods Electric is dedicated to fulfilling our customer’s needs and meeting their expectations by adhering to the following values, concepts and principles:

    * Provide and install quality materials in a neat and workman-like manner consistent with Woods Electrics standards of workmanship.
    * Provide a guarantee on our workmanship.
    * Exercise good time management.
    * Respect the customer’s workplace and its surroundings, leaving the job site in a safe and clean condition.
    * Provide you with the personal attention you expect.
    * Show up on the job well prepared and ready to work.
    * Communicate with the customer by listening to their needs and responding with solutions in a manner, which will keep them informed.
    * Exhibit a professional attitude, appearance and behaviour at all times.
    * Provide cost effective, non-profit driven alternative solutions.
    * Meet or exceed established qualification and regulations.
    * Provided a well trained, educated and resourceful staff.
    * Exhibit honesty and integrity.
    * Perform all work when expected and promised.
    * Establish a fair, competitive price structure.
    * Provide flexible working hours and be available to meet the customers’ needs.
