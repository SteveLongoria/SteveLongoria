    South Sound Electric Inc. is a full-service electrical contractor located in Bremerton, WA. We are local family-owned business, fully licensed, bonded, and insured with over 30 years of experience in commercial and residential electrical service work. South Sound Electric's owner, Raymond Boink, has lived and worked in Kitsap county for over 40 years. He served his Electrical apprenticeship in the International Brotherhood Of Electrical Workers (IBEW Local 46) and has held a Washington State Journeyman's License for over 30 years.

    Raymond is dedicated to making our number one goal here at South Sound Electric customer service and satisfaction. He believes that the best form of advertising is a satisfied customer. This is why South Sound Electric offers 24-hour emergency priority service, because we know an emergency can happen at any time; not just Monday through Friday, between 7 and 3!
